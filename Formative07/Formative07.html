Exercise 2- Report Wizard

When the following dialog box appears in the Report Wizard make sure to choose the following settings-choose Landscape orientation, and uncheck "Adjust the field..." or suffer the consequences.



  • Keep in mind that the Report Wizard can save a lot of time, but rarely produces the final product.
    After the Wizard is done you will need to make adjustments in the Report Design View.
  • Plan on your report output being on Legal Paper (8 1/2 x 14 inches) with .25 inch margins.
  • This will provide a 14 - 1/2 or 13 1/2 inches for workspace in the Report Design View.
  • You are advised to adjust the left and width properties of any object that extends beyond the 13.5 inch ruler mark.
  • You can traverse all the objects on the report in design view by selecting the object from the dropdown list at the top of the Property Sheet and arrowing down.


Beware of formatting errors such as text boxes too narrow that lead to truncated fields.


Make the totals and Grand Total at end of report look nice by setting number format to Currency, and decimal places to 0 (Zero)



Realize that it may take several attempts using the Report Wizard to get a satisfactory report.

  • When the report is done, print it as a .pdf file with the following settings
  • Use Legal size paper.
  • Make sure that the report is in Landscape mode.
